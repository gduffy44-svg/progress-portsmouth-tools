How we triaged Tom's comments

Tom raised more than four points. We worked through everything he sent and sorted into three buckets:

  1. Substantive policy or technical contributions that translate cleanly into the plan — the four below.
  2. Contested policy positions that deserve a real conversation but shouldn't be silently absorbed into the plan (e.g. impact fee abolition, ADU minimum size). Recorded but not drafted.
  3. Process or personnel observations that fall outside the plan format. Worth raising in conversation, not as plan items.

The four below came out of the first bucket. Each is drafted with full pros and cons, sources, and notes — same format as canonical items — so you can review them on the same terms as anything else in the plan.
